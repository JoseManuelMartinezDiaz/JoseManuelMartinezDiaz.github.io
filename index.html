About

Hello! I'm José Manuel, an enthusiastic and proactive video game programmer currently working as a .NET software developer. Since an early stage of my life, video games have been my passion and have shaped an important part of my personality.


As time went by, that passion created a curiosity about how video games were created and developed. I studied game development at ESAT, completing my studies and obtaining a BSCHons in Computer Games Programming at Teesside University. During my time studying, I built a strong foundation in game creation and also learned how to use Unity3D and Unreal Engine 4. With the latter, I managed to publish a game on Steam that received several awards.


Currently, I work in the logistics sector, developing applications in Winforms and Xamarin to help improve processes and increase productivity in different departments. An important part of my work is creating tools for the development team, saving time in the development process. I am also responsible for Xamarin app development and uploading those applications to their respective stores: Play Store and App Store. Lastly, I handle bug fixing in different projects and optimize certain parts of the code to improve efficiency.


Working in a different sector hasn't stopped my need to create games in my free time and continually educate myself to keep learning and improving. In the future, I would like to pursue a career in the game industry, as it is an art form that I love to invest my time in.
